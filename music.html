MY GEAR!!!

I'm not a rich boi by all means so I have to make use of cheap stuff and shit my parents used to have back in the 80's SOOO



My soundcard is a Behringer U-Phoria UM2. Good for beginner producers, but it adds some kind of hiss that I don't like and the headroom is kind of meh.

My microphone is a NEEWER NW-800. Again, good for beginners and shit but the frequency response kinda sucks, plus it sounds dark as fuck.
It needs phantom power; not that it's a bad thing but you're gonna have to search for a soundcard or an external source that can power microphones that require it.

My keyboard is a swissonic EasyKey 49. It's a neat little keyboard, it's got a pitch wheel, a mod wheel, a slider and a knob.
THE THING IS, it's not weighted and the slider stopped working just a couple months ago. Plus one of the keys is starting to harden for some fucking reason???

The speakers are Technics EH750, my mother's old stereo. I don't actually use them for mixing nor mastering, I only use them as reference as to how my shit would translate to the real world.
Plus it's got a lot of EQ options too. If I wanted to properly mix and shit I would use...

My headphones, AKG K702. Probably the best piece of gear I have rn. Openback headphones, almost neutral frequency response, wide soundstage...
The only bad thing is that it lacks a ton of sub, but overall they're pretty amazing.

My other headphones, Superlux HD 662, my first "proper" set of headphones.
I say "proper" because all the past headphones I had were either a fucking muddy sub mess or straight up all mids and the Superlux at least attempt to be flat, but they've still got a fuck ton of sub. Still useful at some occasions.

TIPS AND TRICKS!!!

The Random Ideas Folder

This one has saved my ass several times istg
Basically, everytime I get inspired (I come up with a melody or something) I quickly record it using my guitar or a piano and I save it in a "Random Ideas" folder
So in the case I'm working on a cool track and I don't know what else to do, I just jump on that random ideas folder, choose something and slap it in my track
Within a couple months (or years) you'll have a big ass arsenal of shit you can choose from.
